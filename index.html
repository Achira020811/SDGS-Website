Tech Brands Colors

The official Twitter colors are blue, black, dark gray, light gray, extra light gray, extra extra light gray and white. We recommend using the Twitter color palette for personal projects and in the case of commercial use to visit the company website. The color codes: RGB, CYMK for print, Hex for web and the Pantone colors can be seen below.
